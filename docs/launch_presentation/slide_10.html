Roadmap

Three Clear Steps to Launch

The path from today's feature-complete platform to a live community product is clear and achievable. With the technical foundation in place, the remaining work focuses on branding, infrastructure, and community onboarding.

01
Branding
Finalize the platform name and domain, update all placeholder references across the UI, and complete brand identity assets to ensure a cohesive visual presence.
02
Infrastructure
Activate Stripe payments for live transactions, configure Firebase Authentication for real users, and migrate to a Firestore database for live data management.
03
Community
Onboard the first cohort of vendors to populate the directory, launch a closed beta program for initial feedback, and begin targeted community outreach.
