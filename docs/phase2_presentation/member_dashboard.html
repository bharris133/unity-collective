THE MEMBER DASHBOARD PUTS VENDORS IN CONTROL OF THEIR BUSINESS

PROFILE & STATUS

  • Business Info Card: Displays core details, contact information, and location.
  • Verification Banners: Prominent "Business Verified" confirmation banner for approved vendors.
  • Identity Badges: Showcases the Pan-African Black-Owned badge and Verified status directly on the dashboard.

PRODUCT MANAGEMENT

  • Full Inventory Grid: Visual layout of all active products listed in the marketplace.
  • CRUD Capabilities: Seamless Add, Edit, and Delete functionality for product listings.
  • In-Memory State: Instant UI updates backed by the mock data store, ready for Firestore integration.

ROUTE PROTECTION & REDIRECT GUARDS

The /dashboard route is strictly protected. Unauthenticated users are redirected to /login, and users without a completed onboarding state are routed to /onboarding.
